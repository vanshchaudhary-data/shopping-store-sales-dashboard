# 🛍️ Shopping Store Annual Report 2026

## 📌 Project Title / Headline
**Shopping Store Sales Analytics Dashboard**  
An interactive Excel dashboard summarizing annual sales, customer demographics, and order performance for an online shopping store.

---

## 🎯 Short Description / Purpose
This project provides a comprehensive analysis of shopping store sales data for 2026.  
The dashboard helps businesses track revenue, understand customer behavior, evaluate order fulfillment, and identify top-performing regions and channels.  
It is designed to support **data-driven decision-making** in retail and e-commerce.

---

## 🛠 Tech Stack
- **📊 Microsoft Excel** – Main platform for dashboard creation and visualization.  
- **📂 Power Query (Excel)** – Data cleaning and transformation.  
- **📈 PivotTables & Charts** – Aggregations, comparisons, and interactive visuals.  
- **🖼️ Visual Elements** – Pie charts, bar charts, line charts, and slicers for dynamic filtering.  
- **📝 File Format** – `.xlsx` for the dashboard and `.png` for snapshots.

---

## 📑 Data Source
- **Sales Transactions Dataset (2026)** – Includes order IDs, product categories, customer demographics, and revenue.  
- **Order Fulfillment Records** – Delivery, cancellation, return, and refund status.  
- **Sales Channels Data** – Contributions from Ajio, Amazon, Flipkart, Myntra, Meesho, and others.

## 🌟 Features / Highlights
- 📊 **Orders vs Sales Trend** – Monthly analysis of revenue and order count.  
- 👩‍🦰👨 **Sales by Gender** – Women contribute 64% of sales, men 36%.  
- 📦 **Order Status Tracking** – 92% delivered, with small percentages cancelled, returned, or refunded.  
- 🛒 **Sales Channels Breakdown** – Ajio, Flipkart, Amazon, Myntra, Meesho, and others.  
- 👥 **Age vs Gender Insights** – Distribution of sales across teenagers, adults, and seniors.  
- 🌍 **Top 5 States by Sales** – Maharashtra, Karnataka, Uttar Pradesh, Telangana, and Tamil Nadu.  
- 🎛️ **Interactive Filters** – Month, channel, and category slicers for dynamic exploration.

- **Operational Efficiency** – High delivery success rate (92%) reflects strong logistics.  
- **Regional Growth** – Maharashtra and Karnataka emerge as top-performing states.
